Who We Are

Kinabalu Coders is a nick name for our more lengthy, official name: Persatuan Pengaturcara dan Pembina ICT Sabah (Sabah Coders and ICT Builders Association). An officially registered entity with the Malaysian Registrar of Societies in May 2014, our members consist of leads, organisers, and active participants of the open technology communities and technology-based development industries in Sabah.

One of our main goals is to drive social awareness and increase the socio-economic quality of the local communities through the implementation and execution of creative and innovative concepts, based on ICT and computerised applications. On top of that, we strive to raise the overall technical proficiency of our local developer community.
